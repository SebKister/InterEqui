Privacy Policy – InterEqui

Last updated: March 2026

InterEqui ("the app") is an interval training application designed to assist equestrian athletes in managing and running timed workout sessions. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Data We Collect

InterEqui does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

The only data stored by the app is:

  • Training plans – the workout names and interval durations you create. Stored locally on your device using Android shared preferences.
  • Voice preference – the text-to-speech voice you select in Settings. Stored locally on your device.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

2. Permissions Used

  • Foreground Service – required to keep the workout timer running reliably while the app is in the background or the screen is locked. The service is only active during a workout session and stops automatically when the session ends or is stopped by the user.
  • Wake Lock – used to prevent the device CPU from sleeping during an active workout, ensuring the timer counts down accurately.
  • Notifications – used to display the current interval name and remaining time in the notification shade while a workout is running.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

InterEqui uses the following Android / device system services:

  • Android Text-to-Speech – provided by the device operating system, used to announce interval names and durations aloud. No audio data is recorded or transmitted.

The app does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or cloud services.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its data via Settings → Apps → InterEqui → Clear data on your Android device.

5. Children's Privacy

InterEqui does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a revised date at the top.
